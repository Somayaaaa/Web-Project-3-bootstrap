Pyramids and Antiquities Giza City is most famous for being the site of the three great pyramids of the 4th-dynasty pharaohs, including the iconic Great Pyramid of Khufu, as well as the Sphinx, smaller pyramids, tombs, and temples. The pyramids were constructed around 2580-2500 BC and are considered one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

Paris has a long and storied past, dating back to the 3rd century BC. The city is famous for its iconic landmarks, such as the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum.

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city with a rich history spanning over 2,700 years. Founded in 753 BC, Rome has been a major center of power, culture, and architecture, leaving behind a lasting legacy.
